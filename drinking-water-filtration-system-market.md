<p><p>The drinking water filtration system market encompasses various technologies designed to purify water. Reverse Osmosis (RO) water filters use a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants at the molecular level, delivering high-quality water. Ultrafiltration membrane water filters utilize larger pores to separate bacteria and larger particles while allowing minerals to pass through. Other filtration methods include carbon filters, UV purification, and ceramic filters, each offering specific advantages in removing impurities and improving water quality based on user needs and local water conditions.</p></p>
