<p><p>The semiconductor controlled rectifier (SCR) market comprises two primary types: unidirectional SCR modules and bidirectional SCR modules. Unidirectional SCR modules allow current to flow in only one direction, making them ideal for applications such as power supplies and DC motor drives. In contrast, bidirectional SCR modules can conduct current in both directions, making them suitable for AC applications, such as voltage regulation and phase control in motor speed applications. Both types enhance efficiency and control in electrical systems.</p></p>
