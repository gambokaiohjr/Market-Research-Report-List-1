<p><p>The elastomer bearing market primarily includes Neoprene bearings and Laminated Elastic bearings. Neoprene bearings are made from synthetic rubber, offering excellent resistance to environmental factors and vibrations, often used in bridge and building applications. Laminated Elastic bearings consist of alternating layers of rubber and steel, providing enhanced flexibility and load-bearing capacity, while effectively isolating vibrations. Both types are essential in structural engineering, ensuring stability and durability in construction projects by absorbing shocks and facilitating movement.</p></p>
