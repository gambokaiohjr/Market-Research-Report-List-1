<p><strong>The Manufactured Sand Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mixed Sand</li><li>Crushed Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manufactured sand market primarily comprises two types: mixed sand and crushed sand. Mixed sand combines natural sand with crushed aggregates to achieve desired grain size and texture, enhancing concrete quality. Crushed sand, on the other hand, is produced by mechanically breaking down larger rocks into finer particles, offering superior control over granulation and improved structural integrity. Both types cater to the growing demand for sustainable construction materials, addressing the depletion of natural sand resources while ensuring performance standards.</p></p>
