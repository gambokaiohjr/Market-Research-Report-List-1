<p><p>The Nucleic Acid Extraction Instrument market for COVID-19 includes two primary methods: the Spin Column Method and the Magnetic Bead Method. The Spin Column Method involves the use of silica-based columns to selectively bind nucleic acids, allowing for efficient purification. In contrast, the Magnetic Bead Method employs magnetic nanoparticles that capture nucleic acids, facilitating easy separation from contaminants. Both methods are crucial for isolating RNA and DNA from samples, aiding in accurate COVID-19 diagnostics and research applications.</p></p>
