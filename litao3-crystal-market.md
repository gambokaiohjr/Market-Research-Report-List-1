<p><p>LiTaO3 (Lithium Tantalate) crystals are categorized into two main market types: white type and black type. The white type is characterized by its transparency and is predominantly used in optical applications, such as frequency doubling and non-linear optics. In contrast, the black type exhibits reduced optical transparency due to the presence of impurities, which limits its use in high-end optical devices but enhances its suitability for piezoelectric applications, including sensors and actuators. Each type serves distinct technological needs within the crystal market.</p></p>
