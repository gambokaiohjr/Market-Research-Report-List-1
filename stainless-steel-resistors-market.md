<p><p>The stainless steel resistors market is segmented into linear and non-linear resistors. Linear resistors provide a constant resistance across a range of voltages and temperatures, making them ideal for applications requiring precise and stable performance. Non-linear resistors, on the other hand, exhibit a varying resistance that changes with voltage or current, suitable for applications like surge protection or temperature sensing. Both types leverage stainless steel's durability and corrosion resistance, catering to different industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
