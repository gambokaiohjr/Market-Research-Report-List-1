<p><p>The vehicle lifts market primarily consists of two main types: two-post lifts and four-post lifts. Two-post lifts feature a simple design with two vertical posts, allowing for quicker vehicle access and undercarriage work, making them ideal for small shops. In contrast, four-post lifts have four support posts, providing greater stability and the ability to lift heavier vehicles, making them suitable for larger workshops and storage. Both lift types cater to varying needs in automotive maintenance and service industries.</p></p>
