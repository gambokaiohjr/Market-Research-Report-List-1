<p><p>The feed enzyme preparation market is categorized into three main types: phytase, protease, and carbohydrase. Phytase is used to break down phytic acid in feed, enhancing phosphorus availability for animals. Protease aids in protein digestion, improving nutrient absorption and growth rates. Carbohydrase works on breaking down carbohydrates, enhancing energy release and feed efficiency. Together, these enzymes promote animal health, optimize feed utilization, and support sustainable livestock production by reducing waste and enhancing nutrient profiles in animal feed.</p></p>
