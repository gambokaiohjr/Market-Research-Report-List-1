<p><strong>The Respiratory Suction Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-lumen</li><li>Double-lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The respiratory suction catheters market is divided into single-lumen and double-lumen types. Single-lumen catheters feature one channel for suctioning secretions, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, double-lumen catheters come with two channels: one for suction and another for ventilation or additional therapeutic interventions. This design allows for simultaneous mucous removal and airway maintenance, enhancing patient care, especially in critical settings. Each type serves specific clinical needs, catering to diverse patient requirements in respiratory management.</p></p>
