<p><p>The photo-electric sensor market includes several types: Through-beam sensors, which consist of separate emitter and receiver units that detect objects by interrupting a light beam; Retro-reflective sensors, utilizing a reflector to bounce emitted light back to the receiver, allowing for short-range detection; Diffuse-reflective sensors, which sense object presence based on light reflected from the object back to the sensor; and Other types, encompassing specialized sensors for unique applications, enhancing versatility in industrial and automation settings.</p></p>
