<p><p>Field Service Management (FSM) software is categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise FSM software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring substantial infrastructure and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based FSM software is hosted on external servers, providing accessibility from anywhere, easier updates, and scalability. It eliminates the need for extensive IT resources, allowing businesses to focus on service delivery rather than system management.</p></p>
