<p><p>The automotive rear view camera lens market primarily features two types of cameras: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and low-light performance, making them ideal for clear visibility during nighttime or low-light conditions. In contrast, CMOS cameras are more energy-efficient, compact, and cost-effective, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles. Both technologies enhance safety by providing drivers with a clear view of their surroundings when reversing.</p></p>
