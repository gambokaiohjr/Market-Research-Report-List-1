<p><ul><li>Brushed Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro and special motor market comprises various types, notably brushed and brushless motors. Brushed motors utilize a mechanical commutation system with carbon brushes to transfer current, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for small applications. Conversely, brushless motors use electronic commutation, eliminating brushes for improved efficiency, longevity, and performance, making them suitable for high-demand applications. Both types cater to distinct needs across industries, with brushed motors often found in low-power devices, while brushless motors excel in precision and energy-sensitive situations.</p></p>
