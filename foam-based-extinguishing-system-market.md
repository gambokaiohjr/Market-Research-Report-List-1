<p><p>The foam-based extinguishing system market is categorized into three types: low, medium, and high expansion foam systems. Low expansion foam systems create dense, stable foam, ideal for liquid fires, while medium expansion systems produce a thicker foam blanket, effective for flammable liquid hazards and for smothering flames. High expansion foam systems generate a large volume of lightweight foam, capable of filling spaces quickly and effectively, making them suitable for enclosed areas where traditional methods may be less effective.</p></p>
