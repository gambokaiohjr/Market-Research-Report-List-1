<p><strong>The Veterinary Glucometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Venous</li><li>Subcutaneous</li><li>Capillary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The veterinary glucometer market is segmented into three main types based on sampling sites: venous, subcutaneous, and capillary. Venous glucometers measure glucose levels from blood drawn from veins, offering high accuracy suitable for clinical settings. Subcutaneous glucometers use interstitial fluid from beneath the skin, providing a less invasive option with slightly reduced accuracy. Capillary glucometers utilize blood from capillaries, typically via a paw prick, making them convenient for at-home monitoring. Each type caters to different veterinary needs in glucose management.</p></p>
