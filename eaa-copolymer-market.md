<p><p>The EAA copolymer market is primarily divided into two key types: extrusion grade and injection grade. Extrusion grade EAA copolymer is designed for applications requiring continuous molding and shaping, ideal for packaging films and sheets due to its excellent melt flow and processing characteristics. In contrast, injection grade EAA copolymer is tailored for injection molding processes, offering superior mechanical properties and flexibility for producing complex components. Both grades exhibit enhanced adhesion, clarity, and chemical resistance, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
