<p><p>The injection moulding solutions market can be divided into three main types: hydraulic, all-electric, and hybrid systems. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic fluid to operate, offering power and adaptability, making them suitable for large-scale production. All-electric machines provide precision and energy efficiency, ideal for high-tolerance applications with lower environmental impact. Hybrid systems combine the benefits of both hydraulic and electric technologies, delivering flexibility and enhanced performance for diverse manufacturing needs, catering to various production scales and material types.</p></p>
