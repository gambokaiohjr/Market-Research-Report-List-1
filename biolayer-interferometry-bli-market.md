<p><p>Biolayer Interferometry (BLI) is an optical biosensing technology that measures biomolecular interactions in real-time without the need for labels. It utilizes the interference of light to assess changes in the optical thickness of a biosensor layer, which occurs when molecules bind to specific targets. BLI is widely employed in various applications, including drug discovery, vaccine development, and protein-protein interactions, thanks to its high sensitivity and rapid results.</p><p>The BLI market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing demand for advanced analytical tools in biotechnology and pharmaceutical industries.
