<p><p>Blockchain-based identity markets can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and local-based. Cloud-based markets utilize decentralized networks hosted on the cloud, allowing users to manage their identities securely from anywhere, benefiting from scalability and seamless updates. Conversely, local-based markets store identity data on local devices or servers, providing users with complete control and privacy over their information, but potentially sacrificing flexibility and the ability to access services remotely. Each type balances security, convenience, and user control differently.</p></p>
