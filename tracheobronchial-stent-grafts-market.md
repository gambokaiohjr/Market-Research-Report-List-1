<p><p>The tracheobronchial stent graft market comprises three primary types. Self-expandable stents are designed to automatically expand upon deployment, providing a secure fit in the tracheobronchial tree. Non-expandable stents are fixed in size and shape, offering stable support but limited adaptability. Balloon-expandable stents, on the other hand, require balloon inflation for expansion, allowing for precise positioning and customized diameter. Each type serves specific clinical needs in managing airway obstructions and enhancing respiratory function.</p></p>
