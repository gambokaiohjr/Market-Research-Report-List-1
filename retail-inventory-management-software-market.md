<p><p>Retail Inventory Management Software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and automatic updates, allowing retailers to access their inventory data from anywhere with an internet connection. On-premise solutions, on the other hand, are installed locally on a retailer's hardware, providing greater control and security but requiring more maintenance and upfront costs. Both types aim to enhance inventory visibility and optimize stock management.</p></p>
