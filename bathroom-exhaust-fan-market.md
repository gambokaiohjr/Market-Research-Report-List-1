<p><p>The bathroom exhaust fan market encompasses three primary types: ceiling fans, wall-mounted fans, and window-mounted fans. Ceiling fans are typically installed on the ceiling, providing effective ventilation while blending with the decor. Wall-mounted fans are discreetly positioned on walls, ideal for smaller spaces, facilitating airflow without occupying floor space. Window-mounted fans are designed to fit into window frames, allowing for easy installation and providing fresh air circulation. Each type serves distinct needs, ensuring proper ventilation in bathrooms.</p></p>
