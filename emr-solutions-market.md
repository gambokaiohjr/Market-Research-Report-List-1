<p><p>The EMR solutions market is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based EMR systems allow healthcare providers to access patient data remotely via the internet, promoting flexibility and cost-effectiveness through subscription models. In contrast, on-premise EMR systems are installed locally on a healthcare provider’s servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type addresses different organizational needs, enabling tailored healthcare delivery solutions.</p></p>
