<p><p>The toaster market comprises three main types: pop-up toasters, toaster ovens, and conveyor toasters. Pop-up toasters are compact appliances designed for quickly browning slices of bread, ideal for home use. Toaster ovens serve a multifunctional purpose, allowing for baking, broiling, and toasting, suitable for diverse cooking needs. Conveyor toasters, typically used in commercial settings, continuously toast bread as it moves through the machine, enhancing efficiency for high-volume demands. Each type caters to different consumer requirements and usage scenarios.</p></p>
