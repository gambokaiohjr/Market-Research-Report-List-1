<p><strong>The RFID Elevator Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Offline</li><li>Online</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID elevator controllers can be classified into offline and online markets based on their connectivity and functionality. Offline RFID controllers operate independently without internet connectivity, allowing access control through stored data on RFID tags and readers within the elevator system. In contrast, online RFID controllers are connected to the cloud or a centralized system, facilitating real-time data monitoring, management, and updates, enhancing security and operational efficiency. Both types cater to varying needs in building management and access control solutions.</p></p>
