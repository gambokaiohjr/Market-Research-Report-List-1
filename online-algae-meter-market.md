<p><p>The online algae meter market includes various types based on specific algae indicators. Chlorophyll-a is the primary pigment used to estimate algal biomass. FDOM (fluorescent dissolved organic matter) indicates organic materials associated with algal growth. Rhodamine is a dye used to track water flow and algal presence. Phycocyanin, a pigment found in cyanobacteria, helps monitor their populations, while phycoerythrin, present in red algae, is used to assess their abundance. Each type plays a vital role in environmental monitoring and water quality assessment.</p></p>
