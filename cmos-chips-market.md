<p><strong>The CMOS Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Pixel Type</li><li>Passive Pixel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS chip market is primarily segmented into two main types: active pixel type and passive pixel type. Active pixel type sensors incorporate amplifying transistors within each pixel, enhancing sensitivity and image quality, making them ideal for applications like digital cameras and smartphones. In contrast, passive pixel type sensors lack individual amplification, resulting in lower performance and resolution, typically used in simpler applications such as basic image capture. The growing demand for high-quality imaging drives the active pixel segment's expansion in various industries.</p></p>
