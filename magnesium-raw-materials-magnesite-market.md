<p><strong>The Magnesium Raw Materials (Magnesite) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phanerocrystalline Magnesite</li><li>Cryptocrystalline Magnesite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium raw materials market, particularly for magnesite, is divided into two main types: phanerocrystalline magnesite and cryptocrystalline magnesite. Phanerocrystalline magnesite features larger, visible crystals, making it suitable for high-temperature applications, such as refractory materials. In contrast, cryptocrystalline magnesite, composed of fine-grained crystals, offers improved chemical reactivity and is often used in the production of magnesia-based products. Both types serve various industrial applications, including ceramics, agriculture, and environmental technologies, catering to different performance needs.</p></p>
