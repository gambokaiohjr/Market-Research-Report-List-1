<p><ul><li>Noncompartmental Methods</li><li>Compartmental Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmacokinetics market types are primarily categorized into noncompartmental and compartmental methods. Noncompartmental methods analyze drug concentration over time without assuming specific body compartments, offering a straightforward approach to drug behavior assessment, particularly useful in early-phase studies. In contrast, compartmental methods model drug distribution across various body compartments, providing detailed insights into pharmacokinetic parameters. These methods enable researchers to understand drug dynamics, optimize dosing regimens, and enhance therapeutic outcomes, catering to diverse needs in drug development and regulatory processes.</p></p>
