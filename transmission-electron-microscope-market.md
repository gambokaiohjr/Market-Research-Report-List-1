<p><ul><li>Conventional Transmission Electron Microscope</li><li>Low-Voltage Electron Microscope</li><li>Cryo-Microscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM) market comprises several key types. Conventional Transmission Electron Microscopes operate at high voltages, providing detailed imaging of thin samples. Low-Voltage Electron Microscopes use reduced voltage to minimize sample damage and enhance imaging of sensitive materials. Cryo-Microscopy involves imaging biological specimens at cryogenic temperatures, preserving their structures in a near-native state. Each type serves distinct applications across materials science, biology, and nanotechnology, contributing to advancements in research and industry.</p></p>
