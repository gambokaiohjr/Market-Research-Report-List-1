<p><p>The neuro-endoscopy market is categorized into three primary types: flexible neuro-endoscopes, angled rigid neuro-endoscopes, and straight rigid neuro-endoscopes. Flexible neuro-endoscopes are designed for optimal maneuverability in intricate brain structures, allowing for minimally invasive procedures. Angled rigid neuro-endoscopes provide enhanced visualization in hard-to-reach areas by incorporating angled tips, while straight rigid neuro-endoscopes offer a direct line of sight, ensuring precise access to targeted regions. Each type plays a crucial role in advancing diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in neurology.</p></p>
