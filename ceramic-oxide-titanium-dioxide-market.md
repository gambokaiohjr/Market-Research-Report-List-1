<p><p>The ceramic oxide titanium dioxide market comprises three primary types: anatase, rutile, and others. Anatase is known for its high photocatalytic activity and is commonly used in coatings and plastics. Rutile, on the other hand, offers exceptional opacity and durability, making it ideal for paints, coatings, and the paper industry. Other forms of titanium dioxide, such as brookite, have niche applications in specialized industries. Each type serves unique needs based on properties like UV resistance, brightness, and stability.</p></p>
