<p><ul><li>Pin Type</li><li>Catheter Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil moisture meter market includes two primary types: pin type and catheter type. Pin type meters utilize metal probes inserted directly into the soil to measure moisture levels based on electrical resistance. They provide quick, localized readings but can be affected by soil salinity. In contrast, catheter type meters employ a flexible tube or sensor to measure moisture content across a broader context, offering accurate assessments in various soil conditions. Both types are essential for agricultural monitoring and effective irrigation management.</p></p>
