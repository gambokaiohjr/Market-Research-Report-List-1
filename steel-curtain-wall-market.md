<p><strong>The Steel Curtain Wall Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Mullion Curtain Wall"</li><li>"Transom Curtain Wall"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Steel Curtain Wall market is categorized mainly into two types: Mullion Curtain Wall and Transom Curtain Wall. A Mullion Curtain Wall features vertical members, or mullions, which support the wall system and provide structural integrity while allowing for larger glass panels. In contrast, a Transom Curtain Wall incorporates horizontal members, or transoms, that divide the wall into sections and enhance structural support and aesthetics. Both types are crucial for modern buildings, providing durability, energy efficiency, and design flexibility.</p></p>
